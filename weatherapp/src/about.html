About this project

My hopes and dreams were to make a fully functioning weather app. However, it did not matter how hard I tried to get the free API’s to give me the maps that I wanted I was always provided with a colorful blob… OpenWeather’s customer service team was extremely helpful however and wherever they could be but without paying for all of the upgrades I was left with what I had, colorful blobs. So, I decided to add a fun second page and nix the map page. I hope that you had some fun and I look forward to implementing the Model View Controller format more in the future.
